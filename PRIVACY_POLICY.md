# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 29, 2026**

Ramin Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the **Islamic StandBy: Quran Clock** mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**Islamic StandBy: Quran Clock** is designed as a focused application for displaying religious texts and providing a spiritual clock interface. 
- **Personal Data:** We **do not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.
- **Local Storage:** All user preferences (such as theme and language settings) are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore and are not accessible by us.

## 2. Third-Party Services (Google Play Services)
While we do not collect your data directly, the App uses third-party services provided by Google that may collect information used to identify you. These include:
- **Google Play Services:** Required for core app functionality.
- **Google Play Billing Library:** Used to process voluntary donations.
These services may collect data such as Device IDs, IP addresses, and crash logs for diagnostics and fraud prevention.

## 3. In-App Purchases (Donations)
The App offers the option to make voluntary donations via the Google Play Billing Library.
- **Payment Processing:** When you make a purchase, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. Ramin Labs does not collect or store your credit card details or financial information.
- **Transaction Data:** Google may provide us with non-sensitive information about the transaction (such as a transaction ID and date) for the purpose of validating the purchase.

## 4. Permissions
The App does not request any sensitive permissions (such as access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or location).

## 5. Children's Privacy
Our App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since the content is religious and educational, it is safe for all audiences.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
